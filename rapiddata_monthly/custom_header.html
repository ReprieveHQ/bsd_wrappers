Join Reprieve to help people facing execution, torture and unlawful detention.

Your monthly donation will fund lawyers and campaigners to save the lives of people on death row around the world. When you join we’ll send you a Membership badge, urgent alerts and you'll be on the inside track of our biggest cases.

At Reprieve, we save the lives of people on death row. We expose illegal prisons, stop torture and win detainees’ freedom. And we push to end illegal US drone strikes and assassinations.

But we can only do it with your help. Join us and become part of a core group of Reprieve Members whose ongoing support means we can continue to take on powerful governments and save people’s lives.

You can give any amount to become a Member, it just needs to be regular. This means we can take on cases knowing we’ll be able to keep fighting for as long as it takes.

When you become a Reprieve Member you’ll also:

  • Receive monthly emails with insights from our teams on the ground
  • Get urgent alerts about actions you can take to help stop executions or free prisoners
  • Be first to hear when we take on a big case or launch a major campaign
  • Have the chance to join online Q&As with the Reprieve team 
  • Get an exclusive Reprieve Membership badge
